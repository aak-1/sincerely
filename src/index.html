So what exactly is this?

This is a database consisting of nonprofits, support groups, and other organizations that provide resources across various East Coast states to assist in issues relating to finances, employment, education, advocacy, housing, etc.. New resources and states will be continually added.

This database was formed in response to the rise of anti-Asian violence in the United States during the pandemic. We compiled a list of resources available in the East Coast to connect AAPI community members to their local organizations.
